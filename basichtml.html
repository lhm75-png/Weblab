Visit Stuart Florida Today

From our sandy beaches to the quaint downtown, Stuart has everything a tourist to the sunshine state can possibly offer!

History of Stuart

Stuart was incorporated in the late 1800s, originally known as Potsdam, its downtown would grow over the years yet retain a certain charm reminiscent of a bygone era, a big reason as to why the city contains its character to this day

Residents of Stuart

As of the last census, Stuart has a population of nearly 20,000 and counting!
